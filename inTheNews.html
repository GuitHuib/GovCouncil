The Governor’s Council has through the years been called such derogatory terms as “clown council” but we have found that the problems presented by these elected officials paid more than $35,000 a year (for maybe 8 hours a week?) are egregious. They range from bad behavior towards each other and the public; back-door deals, a serious lack of transparency including no posting of rules or procedures for the public; no primary challengers; and almost always no diversity of councilors, a manifestation of structural racism of the council.
